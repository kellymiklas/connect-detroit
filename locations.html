Where does Detroit need connection?

Although I am simulating data via Capital One's Nessie API, ideally this method (or a similar method) could be used to also map out which areas of Detroit still need to be connected to the internet.

In order to determine which areas aren't connected, I parsed Nessie's example "customer" information for customers residing in Detroit. Regions that have less than X citizens with internet bills on their account are highlighted on the map to signify households that lack internet connectivity.
